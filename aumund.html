Aumund

High-performance and mobile equipment for bulk handling for Mining

Production processes often require an integrated system of conveying and storage technology rather than just stand-alone machines. AUMUND Fördertechnik can develop and supply product packages or system solutions with its own range of products, and extend them with the SCHADE Lagertechnik and SAMSON Materials Handling portfolios, offering significant added value. This is the benefit that comes from ordering a well-coordinated system from a single supplier, although AUMUND is not a general contractor in the traditional sense.

  • Apron feeders & wagon tipplers for mining
  • Chain conveyors for fertilizer, gypsum & filter dust
  • Material feeders (fixed or mobile) for bioenergy
  • Pan conveyors for iron and aluminium metallurgy
  • Eco hopper & shiploader for ports and terminals
  • Stackers & reclaimers (combined) for stockyards

AumundGroup has comprehensive know-how in the cement and chemicals industries, in energy production, specifically in the power sector, in the handling and processing of minerals and ores as well as in the production of iron, steel and aluminum.

The logistics of these goods becomes an important factor in well-functioning and therefore cost-effective operations. The AUMUND Group has its own teams with comprehensive expertise and international experience in planning, design, manufacture, commissioning and maintenance of high-performance technology for continuous production processes.
