Present on all continents, the Eirich Group is a world leader in high-precision industrial processing technology, in particular mixing, granulation, pelletizing and fine grinding, developed over more than 150 years of experience.

With the best reputation in the market, the wide range of industrial mixers and fine grinding equipment Eirich can be found in more than 50,000 installations in operation for the most diverse and challenging applications worldwide, covering industries such as:

  • Fertilizers (NPK, phosphate) and other agrochemicals
  • Metallurgy (pelletizing and sintering processes)
  • Mining (recycling of tailings for construction materials)
  • Food production (cappuccino, bakery and soups mixes etc.)
  • Construction materials (mortar, high performance concrete)
  • Waste treatment (turning poultry waste to fertilizer)
