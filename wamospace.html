WaMospace

HYDROLOGICAL RISK MANAGEMENT

Protecting communities and critical infrastructure from hydrological risks is a highly responsible task for water managers. WaMoSpace provides early and solid information on water accumulation with daily updates for entire basins at once.

  • Flash-flood risk factor updated every 5 days for every place in Brazil
  • Daily data collection independent from cloud or ground cover

AGRICULTURAL DROUGHT PREDICTION

One of the biggest challenges for agribusinesses is crop losses due to drought events. WaMoSpace helps you detect and predict water shortages before common weather stations can see it coming. We provide regional and farm-specifc information without need for additional CAPEX.

  • Water scarcity monitoring without need for costly ground instruments
  • Detecting groundwater changes earlier and accurately
  • Finding water resources using GRACE and public satellite data

HYDROPOWER INFLOW PREDICTION

Hydropower prices are highly correlated with water inflows (Total Water Storage). Accurate inflow predictions can therefore provide intelligent insights into future energy prices enabling early and optimal pricing strategy. WaMoSpace provides continuous daily water inflow and energy pricing predictions for any desired location on your desktop.

  • Fast and accurate data for entire catchment areas
  • Improved inflow prediction through integral measurement
